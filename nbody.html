$N$-body

You are become God! You have the power to create a universe. This tool uses HMcode combined with a log-normal field assumption to produce realisations of the density Universe for different structural parameters. Areas of higher matter density are shown with brighter colours. The region of the Universe shown is roughly 500 megaparsecs across and one megaparsec deep. A region this size would contain approximately 250,000 galaxies. Galaxies are clustered into huge superclusters, being separated by vast voids and joined by filaments. You can change the density of both dark and normal (baryonic) matter, the Hubble parameter, the fluctuation amplitude (defined on 8 megaparsec scales), and the primordial inflationary spectral index. The default values are approximately the best-fitting values from the Planck (2018) data release.
