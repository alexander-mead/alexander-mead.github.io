$N$-body

You are become God! You have the power to create a universe. This tool uses HMcode combined with a log-normal field assumption to produce realisations of the Universe for different structural parameters. The region of the Universe shown is roughly 500 megaparsecs across and 10 megaparsecs deep, which would be approximately 2 million galaxies. You can change the density of both dark and normal (baryonic) matter, the Hubble parameter, the fluctuation amplitude (defined on 8 megaparsec scales), and the primordial inflationary spectral index. The default values are approximately the best-fitting values from the Planck (2018) data release.
